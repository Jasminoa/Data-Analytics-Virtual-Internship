# Week 2: Visualization & Insight Communication

## Overview

Following data preparation in Week 1, the focus shifted to transforming analytical findings into clear and actionable business insights.

This phase involved building a Marketing Performance Dashboard in Power BI, developing key performance indicators (KPIs), analyzing campaign performance, and communicating findings through reports and executive summaries.

## Business Objective

Tech Datalytics Solutions required a dashboard that would help stakeholders understand marketing performance and support future budget allocation decisions.

The client requested answers to the following questions:

* Which campaigns generated the highest ROI?
* How is revenue trending over time?
* Which channels are performing best?
* What actions should be taken next quarter?

## Dashboard Development

A Marketing Performance Dashboard was created using Power BI to provide an interactive view of campaign performance.

### KPI Metrics

The dashboard tracks:

* Total Revenue
* Total Spend
* ROI (%)
* Conversion Rate (%)
* Campaign Performance Rankings

## Visualizations Created

### Top 5 Campaigns by ROI

Identifies the highest-performing campaigns based on return on investment.

### Monthly Revenue Trend

Shows revenue patterns across the reporting period.

### Channel Performance Analysis

Compares performance across:

* TikTok
* Meta
* Google
* LinkedIn
* Email

### KPI Cards

Provides a quick overview of key business metrics.

## Key Insights

### Campaign Performance

Several campaigns significantly outperformed others in ROI, indicating opportunities for future investment.

### Channel Performance

* TikTok Ads delivered the strongest ROI.
* Meta Ads generated substantial revenue and demonstrated scalability.
* Google Ads provided stable performance.
* LinkedIn Ads attracted fewer but higher-value leads.
* Email marketing underperformed relative to other channels.

### Revenue Trends

Revenue remained positive throughout the reporting period, with clear opportunities to improve allocation toward higher-performing channels.

## Recommendations

### Short-Term Actions

* Increase budget allocation toward high-ROI campaigns.
* Expand successful TikTok and Meta campaigns.
* Review Google campaign targeting strategies.

### Long-Term Actions

* Improve audience segmentation for email marketing.
* Continue monitoring channel ROI and conversion trends.
* Establish recurring dashboard reporting processes.
