# Week 1: Data Foundations & Organization

## Overview

The first phase of this internship focused on establishing a strong data foundation for analysis. The objective was to transform raw marketing datasets into reliable, analysis-ready data that could support reporting and decision-making.

As the Data Analyst for Tech Datalytics Solutions, I was responsible for auditing data quality, cleaning inconsistencies, documenting the datasets, and performing exploratory analysis to understand overall campaign performance.

## Business Objective

The client needed a structured and reliable dataset that could be used for reporting marketing performance across multiple campaigns and channels.

Key questions included:

* What is the total advertising spend?
* Which channel performs best?
* What is the average campaign ROI?
* Is the dataset reliable enough for reporting?

## Datasets Used

### Campaign_Data_Q3

Contains campaign-level marketing performance metrics including:

* Campaign Name
* Channel
* Spend
* Revenue
* Clicks
* Conversions
* ROI

### Leads_Report

Contains lead generation and conversion information.

### Ad_Spend_July-Sept

Contains advertising spend records across marketing channels.

## Data Cleaning Process

The following data quality checks were performed:

### Missing Values

* Inspected all columns for null or blank values.
* No significant missing values were identified.

### Duplicate Records

* Checked for duplicate entries.
* No duplicate records were found.

### Formatting Standardization

* Standardized date formats.
* Standardized channel naming conventions.
* Corrected inconsistent text formatting.

### Validation

* Verified record counts.
* Confirmed consistency across datasets.

## Exploratory Data Analysis

Initial analysis was conducted to understand overall campaign performance.

### Metrics Evaluated

* Total Revenue
* Total Spend
* Average ROI
* Total Clicks
* Total Conversions
* Channel Performance

### Key Findings

* The datasets were suitable for business reporting.
* Marketing campaigns generated positive returns overall.
* Performance varied significantly across channels.
* Certain campaigns consistently outperformed others in ROI.
